ABOUT QFC Indianapolis

Our church is a vibrant church that is all over the world. We are a soul winning church and an evangelism-based church.

The church was founded by our bishop Dag Heward-Mills in the 1980s with a few members. But today the church has grown and has opened about 3,000 branches in 90 countries around the world and in the United states. Our branch is the first branch in Indiana established about 5 years ago in Indianapolis.
